## Presentation Tips

1. **Storytelling**: Lead with the problem, not the solution
2. **Visuals**: Use charts, screenshots, and data visualizations
3. **Pacing**: 45 seconds per slide = 5 minutes total
4. **Engagement**: Ask rhetorical questions, pause for effect
5. **Confidence**: Practice delivery, know your numbers
6. **Backup Slides**: Prepare 3-5 backup slides for Q&A
